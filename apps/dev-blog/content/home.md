mosquito.social is a federated social network formed from small communities.
Everyone is part of several communities and groups of people, think of your
family, your friends, your colleagues, former school mates. Think of runners
groups, book clubs, regional political organisations, etc. But also professional
networks, technical exchange, professional get-togethers, etc.

Many of them already use some kind of tool to get organised, at least instant
messaging.

This is where mosquito.social comes in. It's meant to give you tools to organise
your group, whether it's two other friends or a community of 1000 hackers.

## Where do we stand?

We are just getting started, figuring out our mode of work. We also work on the
architectural concepts and the features we want to provide. This site is our
documentation space.

## How are we working?

The tool is meant to be Open Source software, therefore we want to be public
with everything. So here you'll find secrets, plans, ideation documents that you
might not see in other projects. The documentation space will live and be
updated regularly. We als have the plan to give you regular updates in
[our blog section](/blog).
